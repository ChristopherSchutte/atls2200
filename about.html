A Little About Me...

I am a natural people person, I love managing projects, and analyzing data.

Currently in my senior year studying Information Science at the University of Colorado Boulder. I've managed a small business during a pandemic, taught classes of over 20 people, and have lead a project almost every semester I've been in school. In addition, I really enjoy public speaking and presenting, and will gladly take on the task of accurately explaining complicated topics to those unfamiliar with them. At nearly every job that I have, I love to be the one to fix long-standing and difficult problems and take pride in the tidiness of my workspace.

I believe I'd be an asset to any workplace as my problem solving, communication, and team-oriented view make a great at solving complicated issues.
